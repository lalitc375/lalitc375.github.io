Community badges

< gold >

Gold Badges are rare. A Gold badge is the highest award in the CodeChef community. You’ll have to show profound knowledge and ability in addition to your active participation . They are an accomplishment!

< silver >

Silver badges are awarded for longer term goals. Obtaining silver badge requires significant patience. If you have received one, that means you have greatly contributed to the CodeChef community.

< bronze >

Bronze badges are awarded for basic use of CodeChef Discuss. They are easy to earn. If you are an active participant in the CodeChef community, you will be recognized with this badge
